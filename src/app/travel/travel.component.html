The Town

Arcore is the designated town for the wedding and serves as the central location for both the ceremony, events and accommodation. The Church, reception venue and second day pool party are conveniently situated just a 5-minute drive away from the town. For the convenience of guests who won't be driving, a coach service will be provided. Arcore can be easily accessed via train from Milano Porta Garibaldi, and there are ample free parking options available for those who prefer to drive. Please see below options for getting there.

Flying

There are 3 airports in the vicinity of Arcore: Milan Malpensa, Milan Linate and Bergamo, a 55, 25 and 35 minute drive respectively. Bergamo and Linate offer bus services to Milan central train station, while Malpensa offers a train service to Milan Porta Garibaldi and Milan Central. Ryanair flys to both Bergamo and Malpensa from Dublin, while Aer Lingus offers its servce from Dublin to Linate. Please choose from the options below to receive specific instructions on how to reach Arcore based on the airport you will be flying into.

By Train

Arcore is located on a train line to Milan Porto Garibaldi in the centre of Milan. 2 trains run each hour to Arcore, one with destination Lecco and the other to Bergamo. Malpensa offers a train service directly to Porto Garibaldi, whereas a conneting train from Milan central station is required if flying into Linate or Bergamo. You can either choose to avail of the metro service (M2 green line, direction: Assago) to connect to porto Garibaldi, otherwise take a train to Monza, connecting to the Lecco or Bergamo trains from there.

By Car/Car Hire/Taxis

If driving, the addresses of all locations and accomodation are listed on their respective pages. The recommended hotels offer free parking, as do all venues. We recommend hiring a car if you plan to travel before/after the wedding. should you not wish to avail of the coach service. If you do wish to book a car we recommend Europcar on Hertz. Taxis are available form all airports directly to Arcore. However this is quite a pricey alternative and not recommended. There are also taxis services available for getting around in Arcore. We suggest to book any journeys well in advance as taxis need to be booked via phone.
