Arcore

This is the town where the wedding will take place and accomodation is located. The Church and reception are a 5 minute drive from the town, as is Beat restaurant, the venue for the second day pool party. Accessible via train to Milano Porta Garibaldi and plenty of free parking options available. Please see below, the options for getting there.

More information

Airports

There are 3 airports in the vicinity of Arcore: Milan Malpensa, Milan Linate and Bergamo, a 55, 25 and 35 minute drive respectively. Bergamo and Linate offer bus services to Milan central train station, while Malpensa offers a train service to Milan Porta Garibaldi and Milan Central. Ryanair flys to both Bergamo and Malpensa from Dublin, while Aer Lingus offers its servce from Dublin to Linate.

Train Stations

Arcore is located on a train line to Milan Porto Garibaldi in the centre of Milan. 2 trains run each hour to Arcore, one with destination Lecco and the other to Bergamo. Malpensa offers a train service directly to Porto Garibaldi, whereas a conneting train from Milan central station is required if flying into Linate or Bergamo. You can either choose to avail of the metro service (M2 green line, direction: Assago) to connect to porto Garibaldi, otherwise take a train to Monza, connecting to the Lecco or Bergamo trains from there.

Car Hire/Taxis

We recommend hiring a car if you plan to travel before/after the wedding. There will be free parking available at all hotels and veunes, should you not wish to avail of the coach service. If you do wish to book a car we recommend Europcar on Hertz. Taxis are available form all airports directly to Arcore. However this is quite a pricey alternative and not recommended. There are also taxis services available for getting around in Arcore. We suggest to book any journeys well in advance as taxis need to be booked via phone.
