The Town

Arcore is the designated town for the wedding and serves as the central location for both the ceremony, events and accommodation. The Church, reception venue and second day pool party are conveniently situated just a 5-minute drive away from the town. For the convenience of guests who won't be driving, a coach service will be provided. Arcore can be easily accessed via train from Milano Porta Garibaldi, and there are ample free parking options available for those who prefer to drive. Please see below options for getting there.

Flying

There are 3 airports in the vicinity of Arcore: Milan Malpensa, Milan Linate and Bergamo, a 55, 25 and 35 minute drive respectively. Bergamo and Linate offer bus services to Milan central train station, while Malpensa offers a train service to Milan Porta Garibaldi and Milan Central. Ryanair flys to both Bergamo and Malpensa from Dublin, while Aer Lingus offers its servce from Dublin to Linate. Please choose from the options below to receive specific instructions on how to reach Arcore based on the airport you will be flying into.

Malpensa Airport

Malpensa Airport provides a convenient train service to Milan Porta Garibaldi, where you have two train options that make stops at Arcore. One train is headed in the direction of Lecco(via Carnate), while the other is headed towards Bergamo.
You can also avail of bus transfers to Milan Cenrale, but the train option is recommended as it is much faster. For specific train schedules, please click the link below.

Linate Airport

Linate Airport provides a metro service to Milano San Babila on the M4 blue line. From here you can change to the M1 red line all the way to Sesto Maggio where you can connect to the Begamo and Lecco(via Carnate) trains, or stop at Loreto, and change to the M2 green line, direction Assago, which goes to Milano Garibaldi and Centrale.
The other option is to take a bus to Milan Central Station. See information provided.

Bergamo Airport

There are many bus services to Milan Centrale from Bergamo Airport. You could also take the bus (number 1) to Bergamo train station and from there, take a train to Arcore, with destination Milano Porta Garibaldi. Although this is a more complicated, it would be considerably quicker than travelling into Milan and back out to Arcore. A taxi from Bergamo Airport to the train station would be a quicker and easier alternative, and would cost 17-20 euro

By Train

Arcore is situated along a train route that connects to Milan Porto Garibaldi train station. Trains to Arcore from here operate twice per hour, one with destination Lecco and the other, Bergamo. An alternative option is to take a train from Milano Centrale to Monza, where you can easily transfer to trains bound for Lecco or Bergamo. Both Milan Garibaldi and Centrale sare erved by the M2 green line metro. You can find all train time information with the below links.

By Car

If driving or renting a car, the addresses of all locations and accomodation are listed on their respective pages. All recommended hotels offer free parking, as do all venues, should you not wish to avail of the coach service. If you do wish to book a car we recommend Europcar on Hertz. Taxis are available form all airports directly to Arcore, however this is quite a pricey alternative and not recommended. There are also taxis services available for getting around in Arcore. We suggest to book any journeys well in advance as taxis need to be booked via phone.
